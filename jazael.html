Meet Jazael

The Risk-Averse Delegator

Role: Product Designer | Lifestyle: Digital Nomad | Goal: Avoid Mistakes

Who is Jazael?

A product designer and digital nomad who is terrified of making mistakes in bureaucratic processes. He views reading long, generic information as a high-effort, high-risk activity and is highly motivated to pay for a service that offers a guided, conversational, and error-proof experience.

"He noticed right away the suggestions from Clara, and was very excited about the part that said 'Non vuoi leggere?' ('Don't want to read?'). He said 'Yes, this is me!'... It felt really similar to Gemini and he clicked on one of the suggestions right away."

Goals & Motivations

  • Delegate the Thinking

    To have a guided, step-by-step process that asks simple questions instead of requiring him to read and interpret rules.

  • Eliminate All Risk

    To complete his 730 with confidence, knowing he won't make a costly mistake like he did in the past.

  • Achieve Instant Gratification

    To get immediate answers and complete tasks quickly, as "Life goes fast".

Pains & Gains

Pains

  • Anxious and overwhelmed by text walls.
  • Confused by different "Modello" options.
  • Friction from manual data entry (tax code).

Gains

  • "Clara is the best" ("es la chingoneria").
  • Highly motivated to pay for a guided service.
  • Checkout was "super easy, just a few clicks."

The Opportunity

  • Lead with the Conversational Experience

    Prominently feature "Clara" and the "Don't want to read?" value prop as the primary path for anxious users.

  • Transform Info Pages into Interactive Checklists

    Instead of text blocks, ask "Can you present the 730?" with two simple "Yes/No" questions to give a direct answer.

  • Make the "Clara" Benefit Obvious on the Paywall

    Clearly highlight which plan unlocks the conversational AI to remove any doubt during the purchase decision.
