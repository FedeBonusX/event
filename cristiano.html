Meet Cristiano

The Hopeful Evaluator

Trait: Manages a disability | Passion: Digitalization

Who is Cristiano?

Cristiano is a person with disabilities who is passionate about digital solutions but is constantly frustrated by inaccessible bureaucratic systems. He needs to manage extensive documentation and sees potential in BonusX, but is blocked by a business model that asks him to pay before he can see if the service is truly useful for his specific needs.

"Cristiano was frustrated about the fact that for seeing all the bonuses he first need to pay. He expected to be able to see everything first, and then, depending on whether he found anything useful, he would pay."

Goals & Motivations

  • Centralize Documents

    To find a single, trustworthy place to keep track of his extensive documentation.

  • Evaluate Before Paying

    To see all available bonuses for his situation first to justify the cost to his family.

  • Respect Family Privacy

    To avoid entering his parents' sensitive data until he has their consent and a clear reason.

Pains & Gains

Pains

  • Paywall blocks evaluation of bonuses.
  • Hesitant to add family's sensitive data.
  • Blurred/few results are unconvincing.

Gains

  • Finds entering his own data easy.
  • Trusts the BonusX brand and potential.

The Opportunity

  • Implement a "See First, Pay Later" Model

    Allow users to see all relevant, unblurred bonus titles. Require a subscription only to access the details and apply.

  • Make Family Data Optional for Exploration

    Let users explore the platform fully with just their own data. Ask for family information only when it's essential for a specific bonus application.

  • Showcase a Rich & Relevant Bonus List

    Ensure that filtering for "disabilities" reveals a compelling number of options to demonstrate clear value and convince stakeholders (like his father).
