Meet Erminio

The Prudent Pensioner

Age: 55-64 | Role: Retired (Public Admin) | Tech: Prefers Smartphone

Who is Erminio?

A retired administrator who uses BonusX to navigate Italy's complex bureaucracy. While he finds tech intuitive, he's cautious and privacy-conscious, needing to understand why his data is required before sharing it. He seeks tools that offer true simplification and trustworthy support.

"If I have to manually enter my data, I expect more guidance... Being able to talk to someone is something I expected, especially for delicate services like the tax declaration."

Goals & Motivations

  • Stay Informed

    Easily keep up with new bonuses and changing regulations.

  • Simplify Bureaucracy

    Turn "nightmare" tasks like tax declarations into manageable processes.

  • Feel Secure

    Get full clarity on how his sensitive data is stored and used.

Pains & Gains

Pains

  • Suspicious of early data requests.
  • Confused by blurred/blocked content.
  • Wants clearer subscription plan details.

Gains

  • Likes the intuitive and simple interface.
  • Discovers bonuses just by scrolling.
  • Appreciates clear, detailed information.

The Opportunity

  • Build Trust First, Ask for Data Later

    Delay sensitive data requests until contextually necessary (e.g., when applying for a specific bonus).

  • Humanize the Premium Service

    Prominently feature access to expert, human support as a core value proposition of the paid plan.

  • Clarify All "Why"s

    Explain why bonuses are blurred and provide transparent, detailed breakdowns of subscription plans.
