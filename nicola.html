Meet Nicola

The Methodical Engineer

Role: Software Engineer | Trait: Hates Tax Declarations

Who is Nicola?

A recently relocated software engineer who hates doing his taxes. He expects digital tools to be logical and transparent, but was confused by the app's structure and the service process. He wants clarity on how things work and proactive tools to help him stay organized.

"It would be nice to have something like 'We contact you and if everything looks good to you and you confirm, we proceed to send it'. The way it’s phrased here, it feels like it’s going to be sent automatically."

Goals & Motivations

  • Understand the Process

    To know if a human operator is involved and what checks happen before submission.

  • Organize Documents

    To have a dedicated place to collect needed files as he finds them, not all at once.

  • Never Miss a Deadline

    To receive email reminders so he feels "pressured" to act on time.

Pains & Gains

Pains

  • Confused by "Bonus" vs. "Pratiche" sections.
  • It wasn't clear an operator would work on his service.
  • Completely ignored the "Guided Mode" toggle.

Gains

  • Found information clear and useful.
  • Questionnaire experience was "fantastic".
  • Found high value in the Gold plan's extra checks.

The Opportunity

  • Clarify the "Human in the Loop"

    Start document lists and process descriptions by stating "Our experts will guide you and confirm before submitting" to reduce anxiety.

  • Reimagine "Guided Mode"

    Integrate help text directly next to confusing questions instead of using a separate, easily-missed toggle.

  • Create a Centralized Hub

    Offer a "document checklist" or a personal space where users can track deadlines and upload files throughout the year to reduce last-minute stress.
