Meet Carla

The Cautious Newcomer

Age: 50-60 | Tech Savvy: Low | Support: Relies on Husband

Who is Carla?

Carla finds BonusX "very useful" but is not tech-savvy and approaches the platform with caution. She is easily confused by ambiguous interface elements and is concerned about her privacy, expecting to provide sensitive data only when she is actively applying for a service.

"I was confused... I thought the lock icon meant I needed a subscription, not that I had incomplete information. I didn't understand the difference."

Goals & Motivations

  • Navigate with Confidence

    To understand what each button and status means without needing help.

  • Protect Her Privacy

    To provide sensitive data only when it's absolutely necessary for a service she's chosen.

  • Understand the Value

    To clearly see what is included in a paid plan before considering a subscription.

Pains & Gains

Pains

  • Misinterprets UI (lock icon, "Active" status).
  • Paywall is unclear and lacks detail on services.
  • Low motivation to subscribe without clear need.

Gains

  • Finds the platform "very useful".
  • Platform is generally easy to navigate.

The Opportunity

  • Extreme Clarity in UI

    Eliminate ambiguity. Replace confusing icons and terms (like "Beneficiari") with plain language and tooltips.

  • Just-in-Time Data Collection

    Ask for sensitive information only at the moment it is needed for a specific action (e.g., starting an ISEE).

  • Transparent Paywall

    Clearly detail what each plan includes, if services are cumulative, and the type of support offered (Human vs. AI).
