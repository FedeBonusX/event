Meet Adriano

The Overwhelmed Optimizer

Age: ~40 | Role: Service Designer | Trait: Hates Bureaucracy

Who is Adriano?

A busy professional who finds bureaucracy, especially his Modello 730, highly anxiety-inducing due to a complex family situation. He expects digital tools to eliminate cognitive effort by providing hyper-personalized, "done-for-you" experiences based on the data he has already provided.

"I don’t want to learn the bureaucracy, it’s a high cognitive effort. It gives me anxiety because I don’t know. I already told the platform my information... I would expect my 730."

Goals & Motivations

  • Zero Cognitive Effort

    To complete tasks without having to learn bureaucratic rules or terminology.

  • Hyper-Personalized Content

    To see only information and bonuses relevant to his specific profile and family situation.

  • Reduce Year-Round Stress

    Wants tools like receipt categorization to make annual tasks less painful.

Pains & Gains

Pains

  • Anxiety from generic, non-personalized info.
  • Subscription model feels misaligned for "one-shot" tasks.
  • Blocked by technical jargon (e.g., "Singolo").

Gains

  • Appreciated previous helpful operator interaction.
  • Sees value in year-round tools (receipts, calendar).

The Opportunity

  • Reframe Value: From "Once a Year" to "All Year Long"

    Justify a subscription with tools for recurring tasks like receipt scanning and a deadline calendar.

  • Deliver a Truly Personalized "Concierge" Experience

    Use profile data to aggressively filter content, pre-fill forms, and provide context to support operators.

  • Translate Bureaucracy into Plain Language

    Replace all jargon with simple terms and provide clear, concise explanations for complex requirements.
